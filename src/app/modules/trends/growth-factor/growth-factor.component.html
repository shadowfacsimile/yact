COVID-19 / CASES GROWTH FACTOR

Growth Factor is the ratio between the number of new cases of one day to the number of new cases the previous day. The growth factor stays consistently above 1 until it hits an Inflection Point of the Logistic Curve, which could mean the total number of cases will max out at about 2 times from where you are. If it is bigger than 1 you are in the Exponential part of the Logistic Curve, which could mean there is orders of magnitude of growth ahead.
