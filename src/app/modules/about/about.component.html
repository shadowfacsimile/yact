ABOUT YACT

Why YACT?

Most of the COVID-19 trackers I was following were either filled with ads or were information heavy. I wanted a tracker that gave me just enough information about COVID-19 without ads. Hence YACT - Yet Another COVID-19 Tracker.

Sources?

For worldwide numbers Johns Hopkins CSSE github repo. And for India numbers api.covid19india.org

How up to date are the numbers?

Johns Hopkins updates their numbers once in a day around 23:59 UTC. The India numbers update is as per api.covid19india.org schedule. Regardless, I have a scheduler running every hour to fetch data and update.The numbers are NOT realtime.

Future updates?

Just the routine maintenance and bug fixes I guess. No plans to add any new features. Maybe a light mode?
